My Favorite Restaurant is Mehfil

I absolutely adore **Mehfil**, located in the heart of the city of Hyderabad. This restaurant offers a delightful ambiance and **mouthwatering dishes** that keep me coming back for more.

Their **Biriyani's** are most popular all over the state.

I particularly love their **Chicken Biriyani**, bursting with local flavours.
